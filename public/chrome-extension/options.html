Usage Instructions

  1. Visit any job posting website (LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.)
  2. Click the JobTracker extension icon
  3. Sign in with your account if not already logged in
  4. Click "Extract Job Data" to automatically fill the form
  5. Review and edit the extracted information
  6. Click "Save to CRM" to add the job to your tracker
